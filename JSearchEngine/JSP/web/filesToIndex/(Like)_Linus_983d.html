(Like) Linus is the Deftones' first demo tape. It was self-released on November 30, 1993. The album contains several songs from other unreleased demos.[1]


[edit] Track listing

  1. "(Like) Linus" – 3:01
  2. "Some People" – 4:30
  3. "Answers" – 4:40
  4. "Hump" – 3:08
  5. "Christmas" – 3:25
  6. "Plastic" – 2:52
  7. "Venison" – 3:10
  8. "Guest" (a.k.a. "Gift") – 5:49
  9. "Freaks" – 2:39
  10. "Engine No. 9" – 3:48
  11. "Root" – 4:05
  12. "7 Words" – 4:07
  13. "Teething" – 3:48

Other demos exist such as "God's Hands," "Hogburg Hop," and some untitled tracks. A lot of these tracks were written as early as 1990-91 or possibly earlier. While some of these tracks made it to subsequent albums, most were lost to obscurity.

Deftones can be seen in 1996's The Crow: City of Angels performing their song "Teething" during the Day of the Dead scene. This song is available on the soundtrack to the film.[2]
